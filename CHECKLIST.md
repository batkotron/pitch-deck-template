# Pre-Send Checklist

Run through this in the 24 hours before you send your deck to an investor. It catches the silent killers.

## The deck itself

- [ ] **One-line description on title slide** is jargon-free and parseable in 5 seconds
- [ ] **Problem slide** has at least one specific number from research or customer interviews
- [ ] **Solution slide** has a product screenshot or wireframe
- [ ] **Market slide** shows bottom-up methodology, not just a top-down number
- [ ] **Product slide** has 3 screenshots with value-led captions (not feature-led)
- [ ] **Traction slide** is honest about your tier (revenue, usage, signups, waitlist) and doesn't dress up weak metrics
- [ ] **Business model slide** shows pricing, gross margin, and (if you have it) net retention
- [ ] **Competition slide** includes the obvious competitors, not just the weak ones
- [ ] **Team slide** explains why YOU for THIS company - not a CV dump
- [ ] **Ask slide** has an exact number, specific milestones, and existing investor commitments
- [ ] **Total slides** is between 10 and 15. Anything more is too long for a first send.

## Quality

- [ ] **Read it out loud.** Walk through the whole deck. If it takes more than 4 minutes, cut.
- [ ] **Sentence count under 30 per slide.** Trim anything over.
- [ ] **Zero typos.** Run it through Grammarly or have someone else read it.
- [ ] **Australian English consistent** (organise, optimise, colour) if you're targeting ANZ investors.
- [ ] **Numbers all reconcile.** MRR, runway, headcount, ask - cross-check that they tell the same story.
- [ ] **No "lorem ipsum"** anywhere. (You'd be amazed.)
- [ ] **No placeholder logos.** Replace or remove.

## Format

- [ ] **PDF version exists** with file name `[CompanyName]-Deck-[Month][Year].pdf` (not "deck final v3 USE THIS ONE.pdf")
- [ ] **Google Slides link exists** AND is set to "anyone with the link can view"
- [ ] **File size is under 10MB** so it doesn't bounce in email
- [ ] **First page renders correctly** in Gmail's preview pane

## Distribution

- [ ] **Investor list is curated.** No mass send. Each name on the list has a reason they're a good fit (stage, sector, recent investments).
- [ ] **Personalised first line** in each email. Not the deck - the email body.
- [ ] **Specific ask in the email.** "20 minutes next week to walk you through" is good. "Looking for feedback" is weak.
- [ ] **Clear next step** if they're interested - calendar link or "I'll follow up Thursday."
- [ ] **You've checked the deck on mobile.** 60% of investors will open the email on their phone first.

## After sending

- [ ] **Tracking is set up.** Use a tool like DocSend, Pitch, or [batko.ai/pitchmaster](https://batko.ai/pitchmaster) so you can see who opened it and which slides they spent time on.
- [ ] **Calendar is clear** for the next 7 days for response meetings.
- [ ] **Follow-up reminder** set for 5 business days from send.

## The night before

Do this before you hit send:

1. Print the deck. Read the printed version. You'll catch things you missed on screen.
2. Walk away for an hour. Come back. Re-read the title slide and the ask slide. They're the most important.
3. Send a test version to a friend who knows nothing about your business. If they can't summarise what you do after reading, your deck has failed its first test.
